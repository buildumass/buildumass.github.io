About


Our Mission-


BUILD is a group that provides a venue for students to gain academic and practical knowledge about software development, web development, and tech consulting. Furthermore, BUILD gives back to the community through pro-bono services for nonprofits, startups, and local businesses.

We aim to help our members develop technical and professional skills necessary to succeed in competitive fields. Working directly with companies to solve real-world problems allows students to develop critical communication skills and improve their problem-solving abilities. Additionally, talks and workshops from faculty, corporate speakers, and recruiters provide industry insights and opportunities to enhance technical skills.

We are students passionate about solving challenging problems, helping the community, and bringing our school to its highest potential.
