About


Our Mission-


BUILD is a group that provides a venue for students to gain academic and practical knowledge about software development, web development and tech consulting and also a way to give back to the community through pro-bono consulting engagements for non-profits, local businesses and other registered student organizations at University of Massachusetts, Amherst.

From hosting faculty, corporate speakers and recruiters, to developing both the hard and soft skills necessary to succeed, we aim to help our members develop professional skills and gain relevant experience which would give them an edge over others in the industry and at the same time offer a way to contribute towards society. We are passionate about solving challenging problems, helping the society and bringing our school to its highest potential.
