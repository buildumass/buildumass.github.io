About


Tech Consulting for Local Businesses and Non-Profits


We are a community of UMass Amherst students, brought together by our passion for building and solving problems using code. Our mission is to make technology accessible to local businesses and non profits by working in teams and developing custom technological solutions to boost business and promote non-profits.

Our contribution would not only help you adapt to newer technologies quicker but also help you concentrate your resources on other important factors. We want to use our skills and abilities to bring about a positive change in the world and thats what motivates us to constantly learn, work hard, and find solutions where others haven't looked. We take pride in our code and design and make sure it is carefully constructed, scalable and actually helps our client.
