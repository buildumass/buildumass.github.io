Join Us



BUILD is a way for you to apply your knowledge to impact lives of others. You would be working in small teams to build technological solutions for the non-profits and local businesses. Because of our help these organizations would be able to divert their resources and be able to help a larger group of people.


Our teams consist of one Project Manager and about three Project Developers. The Project Manager leads the team and initially meets with our clients to get an understanding of their problem and their requirements. Then, with the help of the Project Developers, the team makes a plan of how they would tackle the problem along with an estimated time frame. The team meets on a weekly basis to colaborate on ideas, improvise and make sure the project is on track.
